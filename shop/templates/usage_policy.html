Usage Policy

What you can and cannot host

Forbidden Usage

Engaging in any of the following activities will result in instant service termination and may lead to a permanent ban.

I. Illegal Activities

  • No hosting pirated / cracked software or copyrighted content.
  • No viruses, malware, trojans, backdoors, keyloggers or harmful payloads.

II. Resource Abuse

  • No crypto mining (Bitcoin, Monero, etc.).
  • No CPU stress testing, attack scripts or benchmarks designed to overload nodes.
  • No editing system binaries to bypass assigned limits.

III. Network Abuse

  • No DDoS tools, packet flooders or botnet control panels.
  • No port scanning, VPN hosting, tunneling or proxy services.
  • No IP masking tools or anonymizer networks.

IV. Allowed Usage

  • Minecraft servers (Java, Bedrock, modded)
  • Game servers that are allowed on your plan tier
  • Websites, Discord bots, small apps (if allowed on plan)
