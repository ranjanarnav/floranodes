Privacy Policy

Transparent & Secure Data Practices

Summary

FloraNodes collects only the essential information needed to operate our services and protect our platform.

I. Information We Collect

  • Email address (for login & communication)
  • IP address (security, anti-DDoS & abuse prevention)
  • Payment reference IDs (we never store card data)
  • Server performance & usage data (CPU, RAM, logs, player count)

II. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No card numbers or sensitive banking details
  • No government ID documents
  • No GPS or device location tracking

III. How Your Data Is Used

  • To manage & maintain your hosting services
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or Terms violations
  • For critical communication (billing issues, service alerts, outages)

IV. Sharing of Data

FloraNodes does not sell or trade your information. We only share data with:

  • Payment processors (for verification only)
  • Legal authorities (only if required by law)
